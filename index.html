About Me

I'm a third-year university student with a passion for exploring the world through my lens. My photography journey began with a deep fascination for nature, and that passion has only grown over the years. I find myself drawn to the stillness of landscapes, the vibrant colours of plants and wildlife, and the quiet moments in the natural world that often go unnoticed. Through my work, I aim to capture the essence of nature's beauty and share it with others, preserving those fleeting moments that make the outdoors so captivating.

In addition to my love for nature photography, I've developed a strong interest in lifestyle, street, and portrait photography. There's something special about capturing the candid moments of everyday life, the stories in the faces of people I encounter, and the unique personalities that shine through in portraits. This passion allows me to connect with people and places in a more intimate way, and I strive to create images that reflect the raw, unfiltered beauty of human experience.

Photography is more than just a hobby for me. It is a way to express myself and see the world from new perspectives. Whether I'm hiking through the wilderness or wandering through city streets, I am always looking for new stories to tell through my lens. I'm excited to continue growing as a photographer and to share my journey with you. Let's explore, create, and capture unforgettable moments together!
